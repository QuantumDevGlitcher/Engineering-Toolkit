# Review Guidelines

## What to Check

- Correctness and safety.
- Clarity and readability.
- Consistency with existing patterns.
- Tests and CI status.
- Documentation and ADR updates when needed.

## How to Review

- Be constructive and specific.
- Ask questions when unclear.
- Focus on behavior and design, not personal style.
- Suggest improvements rather than blocking without reason.

## Philosophy

Reviews are a collaboration tool to improve the system and share understanding.
